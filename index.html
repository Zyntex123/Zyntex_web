About Us


Founded by Mr. Raj Bhalala in 2024, Zyntex is a multinational clothing manufacturing company based in India, offering end-to-end supply chain solutions to brands worldwide. We develop apparel for all ages, genders, and style preferences using sustainable practices. Zyntex was established with the mission to revolutionize fashion production globally. By empowering people and preserving the environment, we contribute to a circular economy that benefits everyone. With 3+ garment manufacturing units and a workforce of over 150 employees, Zyntex continues to lead with innovation and responsibility.
